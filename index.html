A Real-Time Trivia Gameshow


What is Mega Nokken?
Mega Nokken is a real-time multiplayer trivia game played right in your browser. To find out when the next game is scheduled you can follow MN on Twitter or on Facebook.

Is Mega Nokken really live?
Totally. This is a real-time application so everyone will be seeing the questions at the exact same time and you'll see everyone else's score as the game progresses.

Do I need to install anything to play Mega Nokken?
Mega Nokken is played right in your browser. No applets or added software are required. You only need a your Web browser with JavaScript enabled.

Is Mega Nokken family friendly?
Yes. There is no adult content or foul language.

What kinds of questions are asked?
Anything and everything. Some are easy and others are quite difficult.

My answers are becoming inactive before the question timer runs out. What's happening?
If your browser window is inactive or out of focus, it needs some time to reprocess the program so your timer will be temporarily out of sync with the actual question time. It's not broken, just keep your browser window active while the game is playing and everything will sync up again.

Gameplay Instructions

Mega Nokken is an extremely simple game. But just in case this is your first time playing, here's how to play:

1) You will be able to enter the Mega Nokken Colosseum 10 minutes before game time. At that time, a registration box will appear.
2) Enter you nickname in the registration box and click "Enter Mega Nokken."
3) You will be taken to the the Colosseum interface where you'll see your score, stats, and other information.
4) Once inside the Colosseum, do NOT close out the browser window, refresh the page, or navigate to another link. Doing so, will immediately disconnect your session and you will lose your progress in the game.
5) The game will begin at its scheduled time. Players may enter the Colosseum late, but they will not have the opportunity to answer any missed questions.
6) Once the game begins, you will be presented with 30 random trivia questions, one at a time. There are two brief intermission periods: 1 after question 10 and another after question 20.
7) When question is presented, you will have 8 seconds to answer it by selecting one of the purple answer buttons. If you fail to select an answer within the 8 second period, the buttons will deactivate and you will automatically get the question wrong. Once you select an answer, you may not change your selection so choose wisely.
8) If a correct answer is selected, you will receive 100 points. However, if the answer was selected within the first two seconds you will received an additional 25 bonus points for a fast answer.
9) The game ends after the 30th question. The top ten players with the most points will rightfully triumph as the victors of Mega Nokken.
